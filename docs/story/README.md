# Awash Story Bible

## High Concept
A dark elf hero wakes on the shore of a forbidden island with no memory of who he is or why he arrived. Across four villages, dense jungles, ruins, and arcane strongholds, he races a young wizard to find the Book of All, a tome said to grant complete knowledge, including dark arcane arts.

## Core Premise
- Hero: Vael (working name), a dark elf survivor with erased memories.
- Antagonist: Ilyr Voss (working name), an ambitious wizard chasing absolute knowledge.
- Primary conflict: memory, identity, and power.
- World state: the island is ancient, fractured, and full of sealed knowledge sites.

## Story Pillars
- Memory as progression: traversal unlocks also restore identity fragments.
- Knowledge as danger: power without restraint corrupts both hero and antagonist.
- Place as narrative: every biome region reflects a past culture and current collapse.
- Moral pressure: stopping the Book may require sacrifice.

## Main Cast
### Vael (Hero)
- Dark elf, wakes at the coast with no memory.
- Skilled in melee and ranged combat by instinct.
- Haunted by recovered fragments suggesting a past connection to the island's seals.

### Ilyr Voss (Antagonist)
- Young, brilliant, and impatient wizard.
- Seeks the Book of All to transcend all limits.
- Believes total knowledge can impose permanent order.

## Primary Artifact
### The Book of All
- A legendary tome holding complete knowledge.
- Includes forbidden dark arcane techniques.
- Hidden in a deep vault linked to the island's oldest structures.

## Vertical Slice Narrative Goal
In under 10 minutes, the player should:
- Start on the coast with no memory.
- Traverse the first biome and learn Ilyr is ahead.
- Unlock double jump via an island relic trial.
- Backtrack to open a previously gated route.

## Endgame Direction (Draft)
- Choice A: destroy or reseal the Book.
- Choice B: bind the Book at personal cost.
- Choice C: divide knowledge into guarded fragments.
